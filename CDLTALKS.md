# CDL Talks

Every other Friday during school semesters, UMBC's Cyber Defense lab hosts talks with senior research faculty.
These talks involve research faculty presenting their findings and currently developing projects.
Talks typically begin at 12 noon and conclude at 1pm, and are hosted in Dr. Alan T Sherma's webex meeting room.
These talks are an incredible way to connect with the research community and learn about the wide breadth of Cybersecurity research fields.
Use them to discover new fields of study that you may find passion in pursuing, which you would never have known of otherwise.
