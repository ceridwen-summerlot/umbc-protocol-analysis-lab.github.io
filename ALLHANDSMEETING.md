Every Friday from 1-2pm, PAL meets for an all hands meeting. 
Meetings are held in-person in ITE228, as well as online in the PAL discord server.
All are welcome!
There are no prior expectations for anyone in this lab.
If you are interested in being involved in our work, please attend one of these meetings.
We will do our best to give you instruction on how to get started.
If you have additional questions or would like access to the discord server, please reach out to a current member of PAL staff.
